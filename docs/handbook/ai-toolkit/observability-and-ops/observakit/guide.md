# ObservaKit — Telemetry, Traces, Artifacts

- **Purpose:** Runs, steps, prompts, responses, latencies, artifacts per task.
- **Responsibilities:** JSONL traces, OTEL export (optional), redaction markers.
- **Integrates with:** Everyone.
- **I/O:** `runs/**` logs + artifact links.
- **Wins:** Repro, debug, measure impact.
- **Harmony default:** Ship **OpenTelemetry** traces/metrics/logs (Node/Next.js/Astro) and structured logs; power postmortems with timelines and attachments.
