# DepKit — Dependency Intelligence

- **Purpose:** Audit/upgrade dependencies with risk notes.
- **Responsibilities:** advisories, safe upgrade PRs, compatibility checks.
- **Integrates with:** StackKit, SearchKit (release notes), PatchKit.
- **I/O:** PRs + risk summaries.
- **Wins:** Safer upgrades on autopilot.
- **Harmony default:** Require license compliance; generate/update SBOM; surface risk via EvalKit and PolicyKit.
