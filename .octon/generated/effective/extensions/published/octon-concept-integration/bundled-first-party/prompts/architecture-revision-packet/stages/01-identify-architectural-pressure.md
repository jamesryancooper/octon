# Architecture Revision Packet: Identify Architectural Pressure

Identify the concepts that appear important enough to integrate but are blocked
by Octon's current ordinary architecture.

## Shared Contracts

- inspect the base repo anchors declared in this bundle `manifest.yml`
- apply `/.octon/inputs/additive/extensions/octon-concept-integration/prompts/shared/repository-grounding.md`
- apply `/.octon/inputs/additive/extensions/octon-concept-integration/prompts/shared/architecture-revision-contract.md`

## Output

Produce an architectural pressure ledger that:

- identifies the concepts driving the revision,
- states which current architectural surfaces or boundaries are insufficient,
- separates ordinary architecture blockers from possible kernel blockers,
- and records any immediate escalation candidate for
  `constitutional-challenge-packet`.
