# Derive Recovery Posture

Derive the recovery posture from rollback context and retained evidence.

## Produce

- reversibility class and rollback strategy, when evidenced
- likely trigger conditions for invoking rollback or compensation
- contamination or reset implications
- explicit unknowns or operator follow-up needs

## Rules

- cite rollback posture fields when available
- distinguish canonical control truth from narrative explanation
- if the evidence does not support a rollback note, stop and say so
