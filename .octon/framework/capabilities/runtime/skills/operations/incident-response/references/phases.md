# Behavior Phases

## Phase 1: Scope and Severity

- Capture incident surface, impact, and known constraints.
- Validate severity and authority assumptions.

## Phase 2: Mitigation Decisioning

- Evaluate rollback/containment options by blast radius and reversibility.
- Prefer the smallest robust action that reduces user/system risk quickly.

## Phase 3: Evidence and Follow-up

- Record timeline, decisions, and residual risk.
- Emit a post-incident action list with owners and target dates.
