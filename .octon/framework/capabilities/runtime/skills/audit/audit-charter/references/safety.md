# Safety

- Read-only against the charter source.
- Do not patch, reformat, or rewrite the charter during the audit.
- Do not import support from other governance files.
- Do not claim enforceability that depends on artifacts not named or described by the charter itself.
- If the requested task becomes multi-document governance analysis, stop and ask for a broader audit scope instead of silently widening the evidence base.
