# Phases

## Phase 1: Canonical Extraction

Extract explicit charter statements for:

- elevator pitch,
- vision,
- unique value proposition,
- purpose,
- primary objective,
- direct explanation of `what`, `does`, `why`, and `how`.

If any item is implicit rather than explicit, record that as a clarity gap.

## Phase 2: Traceability

Map each major claim to supporting sections inside the charter. Note whether support is:

- direct and explicit,
- indirect but adequate,
- partial,
- missing.

## Phase 3: Governance Audit

Audit:

- alignment across framing sections,
- contradiction and latent conflict risk,
- normative clauses,
- authority and escalation ownership,
- enforceability and evidence hooks,
- terminology consistency,
- success signal measurability,
- dependency resilience,
- precedence resolution determinism.

## Phase 4: Remediation

Convert material issues into targeted rewrites:

- quote current text when feasible,
- provide replacement text,
- keep replacements specific enough for direct adoption.

## Phase 5: Final Scoring

Score the charter on the categories requested by the prompt and ensure the reasoning matches the findings ledger.
