# Safety

- Read-only planning only.
- Do not modify the charter, schemas, or related contracts during the skill run.
- Do not represent speculative follow-on scope as approved implementation scope.
- Treat profile-selection ambiguity as an escalation trigger.
- If the findings source is stale or clearly mismatched to the charter version, record that risk explicitly.
