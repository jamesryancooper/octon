# Exploratory Syntheses

`inputs/exploratory/syntheses/*.md` contains research synthesis outputs.
Syntheses consolidate source material, identify themes, and surface gaps. They
remain advisory until promoted through a governed edit outside `inputs/**`.

## Contract

- Use `YYYY-MM-DD-<topic>-synthesis.md` or `<topic>-synthesis.md`.
- Cite source material inside the synthesis document.
- Do not write decisions, policy, runtime configuration, workflow state, or
  retained evidence here.

## Disposition

A synthesis may inform a proposal, an advisory plan, or a durable authored
update, but the promoted authority must live outside `inputs/**`.
