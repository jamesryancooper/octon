# Risk Register

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Inventory misses a default approval surface | High | Require code, schema, docs, and validator sweeps. |
| Vocabulary preserves generic approval posture | High | Require typed boundary classification for every human-required case. |
| Generated output is mistaken for authority | High | Mark generated and read-model surfaces projection-only unless durable authority proves otherwise. |
