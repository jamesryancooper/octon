# Phased Workstreams

## Workstream 1 — Constitution
Create and cut over the constitutional kernel, precedence models, obligations, and contract
registry.

## Workstream 2 — Objective and authority
Add workspace charter root, mission charter normalization, run contracts, approvals, grants,
leases, revocations, and decision artifacts.

## Workstream 3 — Runtime and evidence
Normalize run manifests/state, checkpoints, continuity, replay bundles, evidence classes, and
external immutable storage pointers.

## Workstream 4 — Assurance and lab
Preserve structural/gov proof and add functional, behavioral, maintainability, and recovery
planes; add lab scenario/replay/shadow/fault interfaces.

## Workstream 5 — Adapters and support
Add host adapters, model adapters, governed capability packs, and support-target admission
controls.

## Workstream 6 — Simplification and retirement
Demote legacy cognition/persona dependencies, delete host-native authority assumptions, and
institutionalize build-to-delete review.
