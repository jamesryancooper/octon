# Risk Register

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Parent appears to own child authority | High | Program structure validator, child contract, and closeout plan forbid parent-owned child truth. |
| Generic approval defaults survive migration | High | Inventory, shared contract, and validator children require explicit typed boundary classification. |
| Generated or read-model output grants authority | High | Every child preserves generated/read-model non-authority and adds negative controls where applicable. |
| External irreversible effects are over-delegated | High | Connector child requires token, rollback, compensation, egress, and irreversibility proof. |
| Cutover occurs before child receipts are fresh | High | Cutover child depends on validator child and closeout requires fresh child-owned receipts. |
