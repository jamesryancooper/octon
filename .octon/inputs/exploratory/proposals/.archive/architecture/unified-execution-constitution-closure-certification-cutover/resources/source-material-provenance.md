# Source Material Provenance

This packet’s resource set is derived from two user-supplied current-state
inputs:

1. a repository-grounded architectural audit of Octon’s current implementation
2. a closure certification memo that reframes the next round as a bounded,
   binary certification event

Those inputs were **not** copied into the packet unchanged. They were
normalized for standards compliance and packet usability as follows:

- `resources/implementation-audit.md` is the normalized implementation audit
- `resources/closure-certification-baseline.md` is the normalized certification
  baseline
- `resources/audit-normalization-and-adjustments.md` records the adjustments
  applied before packetization

The most important normalization is that the release claim is bounded to the
already declared supported envelope and turned into a release-blocking proof
contract rather than left as a broad architecture aspiration.
