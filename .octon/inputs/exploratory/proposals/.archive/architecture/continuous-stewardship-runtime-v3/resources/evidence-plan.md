# Evidence Plan

Retain evidence for:

- program opening and authority binding;
- epoch opening, closeout, and renewal;
- trigger observation and normalization;
- admission decisions;
- idle decisions;
- campaign candidate evaluation;
- mission handoff;
- renewal decisions;
- ledger updates;
- stewardship-aware Decision Request creation/resolution;
- continuity updates;
- final program closure.

Evidence must be retained under `state/evidence/stewardship/**`; generated
summaries may cite retained evidence but cannot replace it.
