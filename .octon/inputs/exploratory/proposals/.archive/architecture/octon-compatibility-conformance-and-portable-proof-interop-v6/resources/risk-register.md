|---|---|---|
| Imported proof treated as authority | Critical | Negative-control validator |
| External attestation treated as approval | Critical | Evidence-only acceptance states |

| External proof widens support target | High | Support no-widening gate |
| Non-Octon system treated as federation peer | High | Compatibility gate |
