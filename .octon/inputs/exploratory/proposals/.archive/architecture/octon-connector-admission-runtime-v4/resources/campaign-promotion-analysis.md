# Campaign Promotion Analysis

## Current repo stance

The live repo says campaigns are:
- optional;
- deferred by default;
- coordination objects, not execution containers;
- not a second mission system.

Promotion requires live evidence of multi-mission coordination pressure such as shared objectives, milestones, waivers, risks, or deterministic portfolio rollups.

## Impact of selected v4 step

Connector admission does not require campaign promotion.

## Future relationship

If v4 later introduces Stewardship Portfolios and multi-program operations, campaign pressure may emerge. Connector admission can support campaign evidence collection, but campaigns must remain optional and evidence-promoted.

## Boundary

Connector operations must not be owned by campaigns. Campaigns may aggregate mission/connector/release evidence only through derived rollups over canonical source evidence.
