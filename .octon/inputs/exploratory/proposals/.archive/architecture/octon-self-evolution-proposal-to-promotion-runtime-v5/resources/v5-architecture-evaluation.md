# V5 Architecture Evaluation

## Selected highest-leverage step

**Self-Evolution Proposal-to-Promotion Runtime v5**

## Why not full v5?

Full v5 includes Evolution Programs, Candidates, Proposal Compiler, Assurance Lab Promotion Gate, Governance Impact Simulator, Constitutional Amendment Request, Promotion Runtime, Recertification Runtime, and Evolution Ledger. Implementing all of those with full live runtime behavior would be too broad as a single next step. The selected pipeline is the organizing spine that later lab, simulation, amendment, and recertification depth can attach to.

## Why this first?

The live repo already has proposal standards and evidence obligations. The next critical safety gap is making promotion and recertification explicit, evidenced, and non-self-authorizing.
