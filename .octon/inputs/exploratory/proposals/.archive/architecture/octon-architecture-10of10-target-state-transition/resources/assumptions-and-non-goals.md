# Assumptions and Non-Goals

## Assumptions

- Octon's current five-class root model is correct and must be preserved.
- Proposal packets remain temporary, non-canonical implementation aids.
- The target 10/10 architecture is bounded to Octon's declared live support universe and does not
  broaden support claims.
- Generated/effective outputs may be runtime-facing only when receipt-backed and fresh.
- Generated/cognition and generated/proposals never participate in runtime or policy authority.
- Raw additive inputs and exploratory inputs never become direct runtime or policy dependencies.

## Non-goals

- No rival control plane.
- No expansion of browser/API/frontier surfaces into live support.
- No product/branding redesign.
- No general-purpose OS framing change.
- No self-authorizing generated pack promotion.
- No removal of the constitutional kernel.
- No bypass of run-contract-centered execution.
