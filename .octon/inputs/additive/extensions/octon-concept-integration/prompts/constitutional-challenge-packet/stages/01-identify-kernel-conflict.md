# Constitutional Challenge Packet: Identify Kernel Conflict

Identify the exact constitutional, precedence, fail-closed, authority, or
support-universe rules that the concept appears to conflict with.

## Shared Contracts

- inspect the base repo anchors declared in this bundle `manifest.yml`
- apply `/.octon/inputs/additive/extensions/octon-concept-integration/prompts/shared/repository-grounding.md`
- apply `/.octon/inputs/additive/extensions/octon-concept-integration/prompts/shared/constitutional-challenge-contract.md`

## Output

Produce a kernel conflict ledger that:

- names the exact conflicting charter or kernel surfaces,
- records the source concept or packet claim creating the pressure,
- explains why the conflict is constitutional rather than merely structural,
- and preserves a fail-closed posture when the conflict is still ambiguous.
