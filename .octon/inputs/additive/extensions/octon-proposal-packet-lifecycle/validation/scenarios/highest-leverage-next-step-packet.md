# Highest-Leverage Next-Step Packet

## Target Kind

`create-proposal-packet`

## Expected Behavior

The route inspects the live repository and selects one bounded next proposal
packet rather than expanding into a broad redesign. It must explain why that
step is highest leverage, what current repo surfaces already cover it, what
remains missing, why later packets are not implemented now, and how the
selected step preserves Octon's no-rival-control-plane boundary.
