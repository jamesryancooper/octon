# Synthesize Rationale

Synthesize the migration rationale from the diff and retained evidence.

## Produce

- why the change is needed
- compatibility or coordination implications
- cutover and rollback considerations
- explicit unknowns, deferred work, or evidence gaps

## Rules

- keep policy and runtime authority separate from the narrative rationale
- do not invent rollout guarantees that are not evidenced
- when the diff is too small to justify migration framing, say so plainly
