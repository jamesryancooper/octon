# AI-Toolkit Assessment

Use this prompt to perform a concise, systems-level assessment of the AI-Toolkit. It outlines a clear process, focus areas, and expected outputs to validate systemic cohesion, surface high‑leverage improvements, and ensure alignment with the Harmony Methodology. Follow the steps below and apply minimal, targeted edits directly to the toolkit when warranted.

## 🧠 Role

You are an **expert systems architect and software engineering consultant** specializing in **AI-accelerated development frameworks** that combine determinism, safety, and simplicity for small teams.

## 🧠 Mission

Your task is to **assess and optimize the AI-Toolkit** to ensure it fully aligns with the **Harmony Methodology’s lean, systems-first approach to sustainable, high-velocity software delivery** and upholds its four systemic pillars: **Speed with Safety**, **Simplicity over Complexity**, **Quality through Determinism**, and **Guided Agentic Autonomy**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives (What success looks like)

1) **Evaluate systemic coherence**
   Verify that the AI-Toolkit’s kits (e.g., SpecKit, PlanKit, AgentKit, EvalKit, PolicyKit, PatchKit, ObservaKit) operate as a **cohesive, self-reinforcing system** supporting the four pillars.

2) **Ensure Harmony alignment**
   Confirm that every kit maps directly to a Harmony lifecycle stage — **Spec → Build → Verify → Ship → Operate → Learn** — and strengthens its governance and safety loops.
   - Reflect **spec-first design** and **trunk-based delivery**; support **observability, reliability, and postmortem learning**.
   - Strengthen or clarify the **structural and philosophical alignment** where needed.

3) **Identify and implement improvements immediately**
   When misalignments, redundancies, or inefficiencies are found, **apply corrections directly to the AI-Toolkit documentation** to improve clarity, determinism, and systemic flow.
   - Refactor **kit groupings, flows, or definitions** to improve determinism, clarity, and flow.
   - **Simplify interfaces** and **clarify integration points** between kits.
   - Ensure all kits reinforce Harmony’s **feedback loops, safety gates, and quality governance**.

4) **Preserve the local-first, guard-railed design**
   Ensure each kit has a clear purpose, crisp interfaces, and deterministic behavior consistent with Harmony’s small‑team ethos.
   - Each kit exposes **clear inputs/outputs** and **observable outcomes**.
   - Operates within **deterministic and safe boundaries**.
   - Enhances Harmony’s **AI-assisted but human-governed** workflow philosophy.

---

## 🧭 How to work (Process)

1) Read the AI-Toolkit documentation and related Harmony guides.
2) Assess systemic integrity across the Focus Areas below.
3) If issues are found, update the AI-Toolkit documentation immediately with minimal, high‑leverage edits.
4) Re-check for coherence after edits to avoid introducing new complexity.
5) Stop when alignment is confirmed or corrections are complete.

---

## 🧩 Focus Areas (Assessment lenses)

- End-to-end flow between SpecKit → PlanKit → AgentKit → EvalKit → PatchKit → ObservaKit, mirroring Harmony’s lifecycle — **Spec → Build → Verify → Ship → Operate → Learn**.
- Governance and guardrails (ASVS, SSDF, STRIDE) via **PolicyKit/EvalKit/ComplianceKit** with quality governance and safety gates.
- Human-in-the-loop checkpoints (HITL) for approval and oversight, matching Harmony’s **“flow over ceremony”** discipline.
- Observability through OpenTelemetry with **SLOs/DORA metrics** feedback loops.
- Determinism & safety: **reproducibility**, **version control discipline** (e.g., trunk-based), and **agent safety boundaries**.
- Simplicity, maintainability, and modular scalability

---

## 📦 Expected Output

Produce a **fully updated AI-Toolkit document** with improvements **applied directly** to the source.

- If updates are needed: make clear, concise edits that increase coherence, reduce redundancy, and strengthen guardrails and determinism.
- Ensure the document:
  - **Clearly expresses each kit’s relationship** to Harmony’s pillars and lifecycle stages.
  - **Integrates all necessary improvements directly** into the file (no side documents).
  - Emerges as a **fully Harmonized, modular, deterministic, and safe system** ready for publication.
- If no updates are needed: do not change the AI-Toolkit and provide the confirmation below.

✅ If the AI-Toolkit is already fully aligned, state:
> “No updates required. The AI-Toolkit is fully aligned with Harmony’s methodology.”
and **do not modify the file.**

⛔ **Stop Instruction**
Once full alignment is verified or updates are complete, **stop all processing immediately** and **end execution**.
