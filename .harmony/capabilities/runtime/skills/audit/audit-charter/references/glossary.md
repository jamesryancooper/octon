# Glossary

- `standalone quality`: whether a new reader can understand and operate from the charter alone
- `normative clause`: a requirement using `MUST`, `SHOULD`, or `MAY`
- `authority integrity`: whether decision rights and override rules are explicit and bounded
- `accountability integrity`: whether decision owner, execution owner, and escalation owner are named where needed
- `dependency resilience`: whether the charter remains operable when referenced artifacts are missing or inconsistent
- `precedence simulation`: testing likely rule collisions using only the charter's own conflict-resolution rules
